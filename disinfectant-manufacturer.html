Why Our Disinfectant Manufacturing

Disinfectants that perform when it matters most

In healthcare, food processing, schools, and public spaces — disinfectant efficacy isn't optional. It's a safety requirement. We manufacture disinfectants that meet rigorous standards for kill claims, material compatibility, and user safety.

As a specialized disinfectant manufacturer in China, we combine pharmaceutical-grade production discipline with the cost efficiency of scaled chemical manufacturing. Every formula undergoes third-party efficacy testing at accredited microbiology laboratories before it reaches your customers. We provide complete documentation — EN test reports, kill-log data, stability studies — giving your brand the evidence it needs to compete in regulated markets.

Our disinfectant product range:

  • ▸ Hospital-Grade Surface Disinfectant — Broad-spectrum efficacy against bacteria (including MRSA, VRE), enveloped and non-enveloped viruses, and fungi. Suitable for high-touch surfaces, medical equipment, and clinical environments.
  • ▸ Quaternary Ammonium (Quat) Disinfectants — Multi-purpose quat-based formulas for professional cleaning. Effective against a wide spectrum of microorganisms with excellent surface compatibility and residual activity.
  • ▸ Chlorine-Based Disinfectants — Sodium hypochlorite solutions at various concentrations. Fast-acting, economical, and ideal for water treatment, food processing sanitation, and outbreak control.
  • ▸ Alcohol-Based Disinfectants — Ready-to-use sprays and wipes with 70–75% ethanol or isopropanol. Fast evaporation, no residue. Ideal for electronics, food contact surfaces, and quick-turnover areas.
  • ▸ Hydrogen Peroxide Disinfectants — Accelerated hydrogen peroxide (AHP) formulas with enhanced cleaning and disinfecting performance. Breaks down into water and oxygen — environmentally preferred.
  • ▸ Hand Sanitizers — Gel, foam, and liquid formats. WHO-recommended and custom formulations with moisturizers. Available in bottles from 60ml to 5L.

Customization & Testing

Your formula, your efficacy claims, your brand

We don't just fill bottles with generic disinfectant. We develop formulations that match your target market's regulatory requirements and efficacy standards.

⚗️

Active Ingredient Selection

Choose from quaternary ammonium compounds, chlorine-based actives, alcohol, hydrogen peroxide, phenolic compounds, or blended systems. We optimize concentration, contact time, and formulation pH for your specific use case.

📋

Efficacy Testing

Third-party lab testing to EN 1276, EN 13697, EN 14476, and ASTM standards. We provide kill-log data, contact time validation, and surface compatibility reports for your regulatory submissions.

📦

Packaging & Trigger Systems

Spray bottles with chemical-resistant triggers, foaming nozzle options, wipe canisters, and bulk containers (5L, 20L, 200L). Child-resistant and tamper-evident closures available.

🔒

Stability & Shelf Life

Accelerated aging studies at 40°C/75% RH for 12 weeks, real-time stability monitoring, freeze-thaw cycle testing, and active ingredient degradation analysis to guarantee labeled shelf life.
