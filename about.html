Our Factory

Modern manufacturing facility

Our 15,000m² facility houses 8 production lines with a combined capacity of 20,000 tons per year. We operate under strict GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice) standards.

  • 8 automated filling lines (200mL to 1000L)
  • In-house R&D laboratory with 5 chemists
  • Climate-controlled raw material warehouse
  • Dedicated quality control laboratory
  • Explosion-proof mixing for solvent-based products
  • On-site wastewater treatment system
