FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

For private label production, MOQ starts at 500 units per SKU. For bulk chemicals, MOQ is 200L. We're flexible — reach out for a custom quote.

Can you develop a custom formula?

Yes! Our in-house R&D team can develop formulas to your exact specifications, or match a competitor's product. Typical development time is 2-4 weeks.

What certifications do your products have?

Our facility is ISO 9001 and GMP certified. Products can be formulated to meet REACH, EPA, and other regional standards. Just let us know your target market.

How long does production and shipping take?

Production typically takes 15-25 days after formula approval. Sea freight to Europe takes 30-35 days, to the US 20-25 days. Air freight is available for rush orders.

Do you provide samples?

Yes. We provide free samples of our existing formulas. For custom formulations, there's a small development fee that's refunded on your first order.

Can you handle packaging design?

Absolutely. We have an in-house design team that can create labels, bottles, and packaging that meet your brand guidelines and market requirements.
