Floor Cleaner Expertise

Why floor cleaners are our core strength

Floor care is the largest category in the cleaning industry — and it's where we have the deepest formulation experience.

Floor cleaning products face unique technical challenges: different surfaces require different pH levels, surfactants, and additives. A formula that works beautifully on tiles can damage wood. A cleaner that shines vinyl may leave stone slippery. Our R&D team has spent 15+ years perfecting formulations for every floor surface and application scenario.

As a custom floor cleaner OEM manufacturer, we don't just offer off-the-shelf formulas — we develop products tailored to your target market's floor types, water hardness, user habits, and regulatory requirements. Every batch is tested for cleaning efficacy, surface compatibility, residue, and stability.

We supply floor cleaner in multiple formats: ready-to-use spray bottles, concentrated liquid for dilution (1:50 to 1:200), bulk IBC totes for filling lines, and single-dose pods or sachets. All available with your branding, label, and packaging design.

Floor cleaner types we manufacture:

  • ▸ Neutral Floor Cleaner (pH 6–8) — Gentle, residue-free formula for daily maintenance of tiles, marble, wood, and laminate. Safe for sealed surfaces, leaves no haze or streaks.
  • ▸ Alkaline Floor Cleaner (pH 10–13) — Heavy-duty degreasing for commercial kitchens, workshops, garages, and industrial floors. Cuts through oil, grease, and carbon deposits.
  • ▸ Anti-Slip Floor Treatment — Microscopic etching that increases traction on tiles and stone without changing appearance. Popular for hotels, hospitals, and restaurants.
  • ▸ Disinfectant Floor Cleaner — Kills 99.9% of bacteria and viruses while cleaning. Ideal for healthcare, food processing, and childcare facilities.
  • ▸ Wood & Laminate Floor Cleaner — pH-balanced, non-alkaline formula that cleans without swelling or warping. Leaves a protective micro-layer.

Surface Compatibility

One floor cleaner doesn't fit all — we formulate for every surface

Each floor type has specific chemical compatibility requirements. We match the formula to the surface.

01

Ceramic & Porcelain Tiles

Neutral to mildly alkaline formulations. Streak-free drying agents, pleasant fragrance options. The most common residential and commercial floor type.

02

Natural Stone (Marble, Granite)

Strictly pH-neutral, acid-free formulations to prevent etching. Gentle surfactants that preserve the stone's natural finish. Impregnating sealers also available.

03

Vinyl, PVC & LVT

Non-aggressive formulations that won't strip plasticizers or cause discoloration. Anti-static additives for commercial LVT installations.

04

Hardwood & Laminate

Low-moisture, quick-dry formulas. No ammonia or harsh alkalis. Our wood-safe cleaners are tested for dimensional stability on oak, maple, and engineered wood.

05

Concrete & Epoxy Floors

Industrial-strength alkaline and solvent-based degreasers. For warehouses, factories, and parking garages. Compatible with floor-scrubbing machines.

06

Anti-Slip & Specialty

Anti-slip treatments for wet areas, high-gloss polish formulations, and combination clean-and-shine products for commercial maintenance contracts.
