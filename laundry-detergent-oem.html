Why Our Laundry OEM

Your laundry brand, manufactured to global standards

Laundry detergent is a high-volume, repeat-purchase category. Getting the formulation right — cleaning power, fragrance, stability, and cost — determines whether your brand earns customer loyalty or gets left on the shelf.

As a laundry detergent OEM manufacturer in China with over 15 years of production experience, we understand what makes a laundry product succeed in competitive markets. Our in-house R&D team continuously optimizes formulations for stain removal, color protection, low-temperature efficacy, and environmental compliance — giving your brand a measurable advantage.

What we manufacture for private label:

  • ▸ Liquid Laundry Detergent — Concentrated and regular formulas. Available in clear, opaque, or pearlized finishes. Compatible with standard and high-efficiency machines.
  • ▸ Laundry Pods / Capsules — Single-dose PVA water-soluble pods with 3-chamber or single-chamber design. Custom colors and fragrance combinations.
  • ▸ Powder Detergent — Standard and concentrated powder for top-load and front-load machines. Free-flowing, non-caking formula.
  • ▸ Fabric Softener — Rinse-cycle softeners with long-lasting fragrance. Available in regular and ultra-concentrated versions.
  • ▸ Stain Removers & Pre-Treatments — Spray and gel formats for targeted stain removal. Enzymatic and oxygen-bleach based.
  • ▸ Specialty Laundry Care — Delicates wash, sportswear detergent, baby laundry liquid, wool & silk care.

Customization & Quality

Formulation flexibility that sets your brand apart

Every market has different preferences — fragrance profiles, cleaning expectations, price points. We adapt formulations to match your target audience.

🧪

Custom Formulation

Choose surfactant systems (anionic, nonionic, or blended), enzyme packages (protease, amylase, lipase), optical brighteners, and preservatives. Our lab develops samples within 7 business days.

🌸

Fragrance Development

50+ fragrance profiles — from fresh linen and floral to luxury perfume-grade scents. We can also match an existing fragrance sample you provide, or develop exclusive signature scents.

📦

Packaging Options

Bottles from 500ml to 5L in HDPE or PET. Options: measuring caps, child-resistant closures, trigger sprays, refill pouches. Custom bottle molds available for high-volume orders.

🔬

Quality Testing

Every batch undergoes: viscosity testing, pH stability, enzyme activity assay, accelerated aging (45°C for 90 days), microbial challenge testing, and stain removal efficacy on standardized soiled fabrics.
