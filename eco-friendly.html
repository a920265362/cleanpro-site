Why Green Cleaning

Sustainable cleaning products — growing demand, proven performance

The green cleaning market is projected to reach $15 billion by 2027. Consumers and regulators are demanding safer, biodegradable cleaning products — and we've been formulating them for years.

As an eco friendly cleaning products factory with over 15 years of formulation experience, we understand that green products must deliver the same cleaning power as conventional alternatives — or they won't sell. Our approach combines advanced plant-based chemistry with rigorous performance testing to ensure every eco-friendly product meets real-world cleaning standards.

Whether you're launching an eco-conscious brand or expanding your existing range with a green line, our factory provides complete OEM and private label services for biodegradable, plant-based, and low-toxicity cleaning products.

What makes our products eco-friendly:

  • ▸ Plant-Based Surfactants — Derived from coconut, corn, and palm (RSPO-certified options).
  • ▸ Biodegradable Formulas — >90% biodegradation within 28 days (OECD 301).
  • ▸ Phosphate-Free & Low-VOC — Safer for aquatic life and indoor air quality.
  • ▸ Recycled & Recyclable Packaging — PCR (post-consumer recycled) bottles and recyclable HDPE/PET options.
  • ▸ Concentrated & Refill Formats — Reduced water content = lower shipping carbon footprint.
