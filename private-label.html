Why Partner With Us

Your brand, our factory — a complete private label solution

We've helped distributors, retailers, and startups across 50+ countries launch successful cleaning product lines under their own brands.

Building a cleaning product brand from scratch is expensive — R&D, regulatory approvals, factory setup, and supply chain management can take years and millions of dollars. As an established private label cleaning products manufacturer in China, we eliminate those barriers. You provide the brand vision; we provide everything else.

What's included in our private label service:

  • ▸ Custom Formulation — Choose from 300+ proven base formulas or develop a unique formula in our in-house R&D lab with 5 chemists.
  • ▸ Bottle & Packaging Selection — Access our extensive catalog of bottles (spray, trigger, flip-cap, pump) in HDPE and PET, or we source custom molds.
  • ▸ Label Design & Printing — Professional label design support with durable, water-resistant printing. Your artwork or ours.
  • ▸ Full Documentation — MSDS, COA, TDS for every batch. Stability and microbial testing reports included.
  • ▸ Regulatory Compliance — REACH, CE, and regional certifications supported. We adapt formulations to your target market.
  • ▸ Global Shipping — FCL and LCL from Guangzhou, Shanghai, or Ningbo ports. DG documentation handled.

Private Label Process

2
